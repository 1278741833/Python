This folder contains 1 years worth of data of 3 data sets for the crypto currencies (Ether, Bitcoin, & Litecoin).
The data sets were downloaded on March 21, 2020. 

The data was gathered from: coindesk.com
Ether: https://www.coindesk.com/price/ethereum
Bitcoin: https://www.coindesk.com/price/bitcoin
Litecoin: https://www.coindesk.com/price/litecoin
